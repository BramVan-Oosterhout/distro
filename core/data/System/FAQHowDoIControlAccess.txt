To control access to your data, you need to understand the difference between
[[UserAuthentication][authentication]] and [[access control]].

   * [[UserAuthentication][Authentication]] is used to identify a user, usually based on a login procedure
      * Foswiki has a default mechanism for doing this, or you can plug in modules that support industry-standard systems such as LDAP
   * [[Access control]] is used to restrict access to content based on the user's identity, and what groups they belong to.

These features are fully described in the linked topics.
