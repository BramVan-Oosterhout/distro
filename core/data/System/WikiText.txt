---+!! What is an !WikiText?

WikiText is another name for TML or <b>T</b>opic <b>M</b>arkup <b>L</b>anguage. It is the native syntax used to
author Topics or Pages in Foswiki. To view the WikiText, click the ="View wiki text"= button at the bottom bar of the page.  This is also referred to as the
=raw view= of a Topic.

To edit WikiText, eiither click the ="Edit wiki text"= button found on the bottom bar of a topic,   or when editing in
the Wysiwyg editor, click the [WikiText] button in the top toolbar.

For more information see WikiSyntaxSummary and WikiSyntax.
