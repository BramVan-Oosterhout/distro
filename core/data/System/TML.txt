---+!! What is an !TML?

TML or <b>T</b>opic <b>M</b>arkup <b>L</b>anguage is the native syntax used to
author Topics or Pages in Foswiki. It's another name for WikiText.   You can
view the TML by using the ="View wiki text"= link at the bottom of the
topic.  This is also referred to as the "raw view" of a topic.

To edit TML, eiither click the ="Edit wiki text"= button found on the bottom bar of a topic,   or when editing in
the Wysiwyg editor, click the [WikiText] button in the top toolbar.

For more information see WikiSyntaxSummary and WikiSyntax.
